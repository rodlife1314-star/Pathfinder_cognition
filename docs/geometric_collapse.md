## 1. The Realization: Moving Beyond Static Dashboards

Most telemetry architectures process data down a flat, linear pipeline. Pathfinder Aperture rejects this layout. Cognitive processing is not a pipeline; it is a **dynamic, contiguous geometric geometry** built of distinct spatial shapes that hold tension. 

A shape represents a focused cognitive state (e.g., mapping uncertainty or validating data). However, **a geometric shape cannot expand indefinitely.** As complexity rises, each shape reaches a specific structural threshold—a **Collapse Point**—where its boundary can no longer hold the next layer of complexity. 

When a shape collapses, it forces an immediate phase transition into the next larger geometric plane. **This is geometry becoming workflow.**

```text
       [ AETHER ]               <-- State: Celestial Coordinates ("Define the Sky")
           │                        - Maps domain boundaries & specialized authority chains
           ▼ (Collapse Point 1: Coordinates mapped, but search space is infinite)
       [ RAPIDS ]               <-- State: Observatory Array ("Locate the Observatories")
           │                        - Accelerates search & filters candidate source domains
           ▼ (Collapse Point 2: Space reduced, but reality has not entered)
       [ HERMES ]               <-- State: Telescope Operator ("Look through the Glass")
           │                        - Retrieves physical evidence records & raw telemetry files
           ▼ (Collapse Point 3: Evidence exists, but is it structurally integrated?)
       [ JEMMA ]                <-- State: Observer Challenge ("Verify the Sightings")
           │                        - Adversarial verification & star-galaxy overlap checks
           ▼ (Collapse Point 4: Sighting validated, but is action permitted?)
       [ OCTAGON ]              <-- State: Horizon Governance ("Confirm Dispatch Orders")
           ├── [ APPROVED ]         - Allowed to execute downstream parameters
           ├── [ REJECTED ]         - Blocked due to safety/policy violation
           └── [ HOLD/ESCALATE ]    - Sovereign action dispatch (Crystal Bridge)
```

## 2. In-Depth State & Collapse Sequence

Each cognitive plane holds distinct, unyielding responsibilities, but does not attempt to solve the whole problem. Instead, they operate in sequence by failing forward gracefully upon hitting their collapse threshold:

### I. AETHER (Define the Sky)
*   **Holds**: Cognitive unknowns, divergent forces, target boundaries, and authority-routing prerequisites.
*   **Role**: *Where should truth exist?* It answers: *"This is a small-body dynamics problem; any legitimate finding must reside inside IAU MPC, JPL SBDB, or peer-reviewed Chandler/Jewitt classification methodologies."* It does not look through the telescope. It defines the target sky.
*   **The Collapse Point**: *"We know exactly which field holds truth, but the telemetry search space is too wide."*
*   **Resolution**: Collapse immediately into **RAPIDS**.

### II. RAPIDS (Locate the Observatories)
*   **Holds**: Parallel filters, high-speed telemetry routes, and candidate authority assets.
*   **Role**: *Where are the candidate sources?* It does not decide truth; it reduces search latency. It fans out across the sky mapped by AETHER to locate MPC tables, JPL databases, orbit indexes, and observatory catalogs simultaneously.
*   **The Collapse Point**: *"We have isolated the candidate sources, but we have not loaded the active evidence."*
*   **Resolution**: Collapse immediately into **HERMES**.

### III. HERMES (Look through the Telescope)
*   **Holds**: Provenance tracking, documentary files, live database replicas, and telemetry files.
*   **Role**: *What does the source actually say?* It acts as the operator retrieving empirical traces. If there are no active documents, the evidence pool remains empty, triggering JEMMA's Law 2 check.
*   **The Collapse Point**: *"We have retrieved physical telemetry files, but does the evidence actually support the client claim?"*
*   **Resolution**: Collapse immediately into **JEMMA**.

### IV. JEMMA (Verify the Sightings)
*   **Holds**: Adversarial verators, star-galaxy overlap tests, and friction indexes.
*   **Role**: *Does the claim survive challenge?* It evaluates if the apparent coma in the image is a genuine comet-like activity or an artifact of overlapping background stellar fields or tracking drift.
*   **The Collapse Point**: *"The evidence survives challenge, but do we have the clearance to execute/publish?"*
*   **Resolution**: Collapse immediately into **OCTAGON**.

### V. OCTAGON (Confirm Dispatch Orders)
*   **Holds**: Policy compliance gates, safety envelopes, and authorization models.
*   **Role**: *Is dispatch permitted?* It checks for required regulatory parameters and hands the telemetry over to the Sovereign Operator on the **Crystal Bridge**.
*   **The Collapse Point**: *"Sovereign Operator sign-off cleared."*
*   **Resolution**: Final dispatch to Crystal Bridge.

## 3. The Two Laws of Jurisdictional Inquest

The Active Asteroids Lab Test (J-LAB-042) revealed two immutable cognitive laws that prevent Pathfinder nodes from entering hallucinations, system drift, or unauthorized computation.

```text
               ┌───────────────────────────────┐
               │    COGNITIVE TELEMETRY FLOW   │
               └───────────────┬───────────────┘
                               │
               Is the Authority Chain Wrong?
               ┌───────────────┴───────────────┐
               │                               ▼ Yes (HALT!)
               │               [ LAW 1: WRONG-CHAIN SILENCE ]
               │               "I am looking in the wrong place."
               │
               ▼ No (Proceed to Grounding)
               Is the Evidence Pool Empty?
               ┌───────────────┴───────────────┐
               │                               ▼ Yes (FLAG!)
               │               [ LAW 2: EMPTY-POOL ASSUMPTION RISK ]
               │               "I have no absolute evidence."
               │
               ▼ No (Secure Execution)
               [ STRUCTURAL HIGH-RESOLUTION INQUIRY ]
```

### Law 1: Wrong-Chain Silence (Authority Misrouting)
> *"Retrieval without correct jurisdiction is system noise. An inquiry target must be queried only under its legal authority chain, or the node must remain silent."*

*   **Mechanics**: If a query is targeted at an object but references generalist database authorities in place of specialized coordinates (e.g., using Simbad catalogs for active minor planets instead of the MPC or JPL Solar System Dynamics database), JEMMA triggers a **Jurisdictional Boundary Violation**.
*   **Resolution**: The node must refuse to analyze or make claims on the raw telemetry until the correct authority chain is formally resolved. It chooses to say: *"I am looking in the wrong place,"* rather than simulating predictions on inappropriate catalogs.

### Law 2: Empty Evidence Pool (Assumption Risk)
> *"Retrieval without physical documentary assets is fabrication. If HERMES contains no verified records, all derived conclusions are flagged permanently as assumed risk."*

*   **Mechanics**: JEMMA monitors the HERMES evidence pipeline. If the pool is completely empty, the system blocks the generation of any authoritative findings.
*   **Resolution**: Any pathways processed default to absolute "unsubstantiated" assumptions. The system alerts the Operator: *"I have no evidence,"* preventing the system from producing confident-sounding summaries based on general hallucinated weights rather than physical documents.

## 4. Summary: The Compass vs. The Telescope

By separating **Jurisdiction (AETHER / Authority Chain)** from **Grounding (HERMES / Evidence)**, Pathfinder Aperture separates:
1.  **Unknown**: *"We don't know the parameters."* (AETHER)
2.  **Unproven**: *"We know the parameters, but we have no files."* (HERMES)
3.  **False**: *"We have the files, and they contradict the claim."* (JEMMA)

This represents the ultimate triumph of the **Cognitive Compass** over the **Technological Telescope**. The glass only amplifies; the doctrine decides.

## 1. JEMMA's Core Postulate: The Ordering Warning

In conventional systems, technology is deployed first, and doctrine is written afterward in a desperate attempt to limit the fallout. JEMMA flags this as a **Topological Inversion Error**. 

When technology precedes doctrine, raw computation begins to dictate what information is surfaced, in what order, and with what underlying biases. The telescope decides where the observer looks, and soon, the observer forgets how to read the stars with the naked eye.

Under the **Sovereign Observer Aperture**, the sequence is absolute and unidirectional:

```text
       [ DOCTRINE ]       <-- The Compass (Determines why and how we look)
            ↓
       [ GOVERNANCE ]     <-- The Filter (Enforces boundary rules)
            ↓
      [ TECHNOLOGY ]      <-- The Telescope (Magnifies reality at velocity)
```

The technology does not think for the pilot. It increases the pilot's visual acuity, projecting patterns out of chaotic noise so the pilot can exercise *true sovereign decision*.

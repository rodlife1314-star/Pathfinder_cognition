# Pathfinder Aperture Cognitive Vocabulary

This document serves as the absolute, single source of truth for terms used within the Pathfinder Aperture repository. It provides strict semantic alignments to prevent conceptual drift between internal cognitive layers and user-facing representations.

---

## The Core Lexicon

### Aperture
The structural opening through which raw, chaotic telemetry arrays are scoped and converted into a visible, coherent coordinate layout.

### Operator
The human sovereign at the absolute center of the cognitive environment. The final authority, standing with two feet on the floor, holding direct execution and validation custody.

### Structure
Objective, mathematical patterns, relationships, correlations, and densities resolved from raw, unaligned telemetry (typically processed at scale via NVIDIA RAPIDS).

### Intelligence
The functional interpretation of verified structure mapped to possible alternatives, systems, and actions. *Intelligence is structured understanding, not mere calculation.*

### Consciousness
Not defined or simulated by this repository.

### Governance
The objective constraint and rule enforcement system (enforced pre-commit by OCTAGON) that operates independently of proposed outcomes or postures.

### Sovereignty
The final, non-delegable authority retained exclusively by the human Operator. Subsystems assist and governance constrains, but the operator ultimately decides.

## Spatial & Geometric Taxonomy

### Geometry
The topological arrangement of cognitive layers (AETHER through CRYSTAL BRIDGE) as distances, areas, and orientation vectors radiating from the operator-at-the-center, rather than flat dashboard blocks.

### Field
The outer boundary of raw, chaotic, unanchored telemetry lines before filtering, processing, or authorization sweeps.

### Spectrum
The refracted, multi-dimensional display of clean, resolved structures, alternative trajectories, and compliance scores arranged logically around the observer's focus line.

### Horizon
The perimeter of validated physical evidence currently available, indexed, and tracked. Anything lying beyond this horizon is flagged as an substantiated assumption.

### Representational Drift
The undesirable shift where deep cognitive geometries are simplified or "flattened" back into administrative UI objects, tables, or generic lists.

### The Compass
The supreme, unchanging regulatory layer of Doctrine and Operator Sovereignty. The Compass dictates *why* and *how* we choose to look, remaining completely independent of technological capabilities.

### The Telescope
The deep instrumentation and physical hardware layers (e.g., NVIDIA RAPIDS VRAM processors, Gemini API grounding pipelines). The Telescope exists only to magnify reality feed resolution, sitting subordinate beneath the Compass. See `/docs/telescope_doctrine.md` for the JEMMA challenge schema.

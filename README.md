<# Pathfinder Aperture

> Structure at velocity. Sovereignty at the center.

Pathfinder Aperture is a GPU-accelerated cognitive telemetry architecture designed to separate high-speed structural resolution from governance-bound reasoning.

The system is built around a simple principle:

Raw data is not intelligence.

Intelligence emerges when verified structure, challenge, governance, and operator judgement are combined into a traceable decision process.

---

## Core Purpose

Pathfinder Aperture exists to transform high-volume telemetry into operator-usable understanding while preserving evidence integrity and decision sovereignty.

The architecture is divided into two layers:

### Fast Path
Responsible for structural resolution.

- NVIDIA RAPIDS
- cuDF
- cuML
- cuGraph
- Stream telemetry
- Statistical compression

The fast path answers:

> What structure exists inside the data?

---

### Slow Path
Responsible for interpretation, challenge, governance, and action.

The slow path answers:

> What does the structure mean?

---

# Cognitive Architecture

## AETHER

Purpose:

Hold uncertainty.

AETHER maps unknowns before assumptions are allowed to form.

Question:

> What do we not yet know?

---

## HERMES

Purpose:

Evidence retrieval and grounding.

Every claim must trace back to a source.

Question:

> What evidence exists?

---

## RAPIDS

Purpose:

Verified structural resolution.

RAPIDS operates as the system aperture.

It compresses large-scale telemetry into high-density structural signatures.

Question:

> What patterns are objectively present?

Output:

- Structural vectors
- Statistical shifts
- Correlation signals
- Topological relationships

RAPIDS does not reason.

RAPIDS resolves.

---

## SIMON

Purpose:

Functional interpretation.

SIMON translates structural relationships into understandable system behaviour.

Question:

> What function does this structure perform?

SIMON does not create data.

SIMON interprets verified structure.

---

## JEMMA

Purpose:

Challenge and contradiction discovery.

JEMMA is the challenge layer.

Question:

> What if we are wrong?

Responsibilities:

- Surface contradictions
- Detect authority gaps
- Identify feed gaps
- Expose assumptions
- Generate friction reports

JEMMA does not resolve contradictions.

JEMMA exposes them.

---

## DELTA

Purpose:

Cognitive acquisition.

DELTA bridges the gap between validated information and operator understanding.

Question:

> What has actually been learned?

Input:

- Findings
- Contradictions
- Friction packets
- Evidence chains

Output:

- Acquired principles
- Internalized understanding
- Transferable knowledge

DELTA does not decide.

DELTA measures acquisition.

---

## OCTAGON

Purpose:

Governance.

Question:

> Is this permitted?

OCTAGON enforces:

- Authority chains
- Coverage requirements
- Validation requirements
- Operator protections

---

## CRYSTAL BRIDGE

Purpose:

Controlled action dispatch.

Question:

> Should this leave the system?

No dispatch occurs without operator authorization.

---
